Mayada El-Henawy

The singing cat

The syrian singer gained most of her fame due to her working with baligh in many songs which he wrote for her to sing and she did more than fine which impressed the public back then

Mayada gained even more fame recently among the youth who seems to like many of her songs and admires her unique voice
